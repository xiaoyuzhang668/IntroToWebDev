Guangzhou, China

Location

Located on the Pearl River about 120 km (75 mi) north-northwest of Hong Kong and 145 km (90 mi) north of Macau

About

Weather

Guangzhou weather is generally warm and humid all year round without a clear division of seasons.

Population

As of the 2020 census, the registered population of the city's expansive administrative area was 18,676,605 individuals.

Sports

Popular amateur sports include table tennis, badminton, martial arts, and various forms of pool.

Things To Do

  • Visit Chimelong Safari Resort
  • Ascend Canton Tower
  • Sightsee on Shamian Island
  • Visit the Chen Clan Ancestral Hall
  • Watch an Artistic Performance in Guangzhou Opera House
  • Visit Zhujiang New Town
  • Visit the Sacred Heart Cathedral
  • Visit the Statue of Five Goats in Yuexiu Park
  • Get Out to Baiyun Mountain Park
  • Take a Pearl River Cruise
